About Me

My passion lies in creative problem solving. I do this by creating creative applications that solve an issue that I encounter. When I worked at a VR Arcade, we had an issue with customers not knowing our catalog of games that were available to be played. I created on my own initiative an ASP.NET web application that would show case the games trailers.

I love to learn new skills and concepts, that is why I am currently studying Business Technology Management at Ryerson University. I also like to take initiative and self-study, I spent a couple of months learning Unity Game Engine and then created my first mobile game DuetJump, which I then self-published on the play store.
